Data protection in the event of a software update for your device, according to its compatibility with this service:

In order to provide you with the very best service, your device will automatically check for/obtain software update information from the manufacturer and its partners after connecting to the Internet. This process may use mobile data, and requires access to your device's identification data, such as its IMEI number, serial number (SN) and your network operator's ID (PLMN), in order to verify whether your device can be updated. This data is collected for software update purposes only and will not be subject to any further processing.

Personal Data Protection as part of the procedure to return your device to your operator:

Whatever the cause of return of your device to your operator or the associated authorized service center, please note that, by default, any personal data contained in the device will be systematically and irremediably erased and therefore will not be processed further. The only personal data we store are those collected and communicated by your operator which we will only use for the purpose of repair.
